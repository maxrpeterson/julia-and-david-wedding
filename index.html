Food & Attire

The wedding invite does indeed say that our event will be a summer picnic. We cannot of course plan on temperature and weather conditions so please consult current weather forecasts as the time draws near. To help you navigate the big question of what to wear, we thought some examples might help.

This is a very casual picnic. We will be sitting at picnic tables. There will be outdoor/backyard games. We hope it will feel like the Delaware Saengerbund Oktoberfest as much as possible (since that was the beginning of our relationship). So, wear what you would wear for that…
